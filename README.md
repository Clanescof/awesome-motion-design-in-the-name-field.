# Awesome Motion Design Resources 2026
A curated list of inspiration, tools, and learning paths for motion designers.

### Inspiration & Showcases
* [Motionographer](https://motionographer.com) - The industry standard for motion news.
* [Stash Media](https://www.stashmedia.tv) - Premier library of animation and VFX.
* [Art of the Title](https://www.artofthetitle.com) - Best resource for film and TV title sequences.
* [Vimeo Staff Picks](https://vimeo.com/channels/staffpicks) - High-end short films and animation.

### 2026 Industry Tools
* [After Effects](https://www.adobe.com/products/aftereffects.html) - Still the king of 2D.
* [Blender](https://www.blender.org) - Open-source 3D motion design.
* [Runway](https://runwayml.com) - AI-powered video generation and editing.
* [Cavalry](https://scenegroup.co) - Real-time 2D animation for procedural design.

### Learning
* [School of Motion](https://www.schoolofmotion.com) - Professional-grade courses.
* [Ben Marriott](https://www.youtube.com/@BenMarriott) - Essential YouTube tutorials..
